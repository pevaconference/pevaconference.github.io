We cordially invite you to the unique conference that has become a regular event in calendar of Polish equine veterinary medicine, the XVII INTERNATIONAL EQUINE VETERINARY CONFERENCE OF THE POLISH EQUINE VETERINARY ASSOCIATION, which this year will take place on:

November 14-15, 2024, at the HOTEL REMES SPORT & SPA****
ul. Parkowa 48, 64-330 Opalenica.

*This year's conference will be preceded by the VetPD course on November 15-16, held at the Equi Vet Serwis clinic, 15 minutes from the REMES hotel.
Course topic: Advances in Foot Lameness & Farriery – Lectures (online), On-Location Case Discussions & Practical Sessions.

For the seventeenth time, supported by the best specialists from around the world, we will have the pleasure of hosting you at our conference. The conference topics, chosen by the organizing committee, will cover a wide range of issues related to equine diagnostic imaging.

This year two of the lectures will be dedicated to building your personal brand, including promoting yourself and your skills. We will focus on self-development and professional burnout prevention, as it is the key element of your professional success and job satisfaction.

See you in November!

OUR SPEAKERS

Dr Mahmoud Mageed Dipl. ECVDI
Mahmoud Mageed earned his Bachelor’s degree in Veterinary Medicine in 2007. He worked as an assistant radiologist for 5 years at the University of Leipzig’s equine clinic, where he obtained his Dr. med. vet. degree in 2014. Mahmoud worked as a senior radiologist in a private clinic for nearly a decade, where he was responsible for image acquisition and interpretation, primarily MRI and CT. In 2018, Mahmoud achieved the status of a German board-certified specialist in Diagnostic Imaging. In 2022, he began his ECVDI residency in collaboration with the University of Zurich.
Mahmoud has published more than 20 publications in the field of diagnostic imaging.
Marianna Biggi DVM, PHD, FHEA, DipECVDI, MRCVS European and RCVS Veterinary Surgeon
Dr Marianna Biggi received her degree in veterinary medicine in 2004 from the University of Parma. After one year in equine practice she started her PhD in Equine diagnostic imaging at the University of Milan, in collaboration with the Animal Health Trust, and completed it in 2010. She subsequently completed a residency program at the Animal Health Trust (UK) obtaining the diploma in Large Animal diagnostic imaging in 2015. More recently she was lecturer in large animal radiology at the Royal Veterinary College (UK)) and joined Pool House Equine Hospital in June 2021.
Tomasz Zawadzki
General Manager of MindSonar® Poland. With 20 years of experience as a soft skills trainer, collaborating with global training companies such as Richardson (USA) and Relevance (Netherlands), as well as a coach. Certified facilitator of Relational Presence®/Speaking Circles® – a method of public speaking based on mindfulness in interaction with the audience. As a manager, he has managed networks in the banking industry. He co-created and implemented recruitment profiles, coaching models for networks, work standardization, remuneration systems, reporting systems, and leadership behaviors. He holds a postgraduate degree in "Coaching in the Organization" from the Faculty of Psychology at the University of Warsaw. He has completed courses in Transforming Communication – conflict resolution, Provocative Coaching, Practitioner NLP, Team Coaching, and is a certified Relational Presence facilitator. Training experience includes numerous projects for employees of Polish and international companies; he has also lectured at universities (UEW) and MBA programs (GFKM).
Dagmara Kołodziejczyk
She helps leaders enhance their leadership skills, enables teams to achieve synergy, and supports the strengthening of self-confidence and self-esteem. Trains management, sales, production, and HR teams. Advises on HRM projects and the development of innovative organizational culture.
She has 20 years of professional, managerial, and psychological experience in human resources management. She has conducted over 2,500 hours of coaching sessions—individual, business, team, and group—as well as trainings and workshops for the pharmaceutical, production, trade, forwarding, and logistics industries.
Work is her passion. She recognizes the holistic and systemic context of clients and organizations. She writes and publishes articles on management, entrepreneurship, coaching, and systemic approaches in business. She co-creates the programs "The Real Me" and "Conscious Man."
She is a mother, partner, dreamer, amateur dancer, and traveler.
Przemysław Lelewski
Guides organizations through change and restructuring. Co-creates business strategies. Leads development and advisory projects for HR, HRBP, sales forces, and customer experience. Trains in coaching techniques, team coaching, and train-the-trainer programs. Conducts individual executive coaching processes.
He has many years of training experience. He is a promoter and co-creator of the Edutainment Trainer Academy. He collaborates with academic institutions such as the Kozminski University, Jagiellonian University, Warsaw School of Economics, and WSB Universities.
He specializes in building effective teams, motivational management, and leadership skills. He introduces virtual reality elements into business training, uses sociological and psychological knowledge, and has experience as a first aid instructor.
He loves the forest, water, and his work, which allows him to meet people and explore the world.
